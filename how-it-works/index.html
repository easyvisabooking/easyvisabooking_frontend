How Our US Visa Appointment Booking Service Works

Our US visa appointment booking process is simple and fully automated. Whether you are in Dubai, Canada, or anywhere worldwide, here is exactly what happens after you contact us. Our visa slot monitoring system continuously tracks availability.

Our Process

Step-by-Step Booking Process

Step 01
Client Details Shared
Your client provides their portal login credentials, DS-160 reference, preferred consulate, and available date range for the appointment. We also require any specific preferences regarding morning or afternoon slots to better tailor our monitoring. Our team ensures that all provided information is handled securely and used only for the purpose of securing your appointment.
Login credentials Preferred dates Consulate
Step 02
We Configure the System
We set up the client's profile on our cloud system AIS or CGI portal, depending on their visa category and consulate location. No action needed from you. Our advanced algorithms are optimized for both major portals to ensure maximum efficiency and speed during the booking process. This setup phase is crucial for enabling real-time responses to slot openings as they happen.
AIS portal CGI portal Profile setup
Step 03
24/7 Automated Monitoring
Our system runs continuously on our servers checking for slot availability every few seconds. No manual checking, no laptop needed. Always on. This persistent monitoring covers even the odd hours when manual checking would be impossible, significantly increasing the chances of finding early cancellations. We use distributed server locations to ensure that our system maintains a constant connection to the appointment portals.
Real-time tracking Cloud-based Always on
Step 04
Slot Booked. You're Notified.
The moment a slot opens within the requested range, we book it instantly. Your client receives confirmation email, SMS, and WhatsApp update. Once the booking is successful, we provide a complete confirmation receipt that you can share directly with your client for their records. You can then focus on preparing the applicant for their interview while we handle any potential rescheduling if a better date opens up.
Instant booking Email confirmation WhatsApp update
